Research

Broadly speaking, I am interested in understanding the fundamental laws of nature, in both the smallest and the largest scales. In physics, these are typically classified as particle physics which studies the smallest fundamental particles that consist the universe and mediate fundamental forces, and cosmology which studies the largest-sacle dynamics of the universe and its history.

What is fascinating is that the study of the two regimes are not unrelated, but in fact closely knit together. Astronomical observations suggest the existence of dark matter and dark energy, which hint at potential imperfections in particle physics and quantum field theory.

In addition to teaching, I also enjoy counseling/advising—really just talking & listening to people and sharing my experiences.

I have been blessed with many good mentors and leaders throughout my life, especially academically. I would love to pay back by being a good role model to others and sharing my experiences.
