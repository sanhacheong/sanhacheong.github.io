Teaching

Outside of my own research, I am very passionate about teaching science and math. It is truly satisfying to be able to help others understand difficult material and, more importantly, get them to enjoy the subjects. Also, teaching often is a great opportunity to review the material myself and really think about the overall picture.

Thankfully, being a teaching assistant since my sophomore year of undergrad, I have had a lot of opportunities to teach other students at college level. Here are the classes I have taught so far:

In addition to teaching, I also enjoy counseling/advising—really just talking & listening to people and sharing my experiences.

I have been blessed with many good mentors and leaders throughout my life, especially academically. I would love to pay back by being a good role model to others and sharing my experiences.
